HAPPY HOUR

Goat Town is a New York City neighborhood restaurant opened in the East Village in 2010.
We serve food and beverage that is focused on quality products from near and far.
The menus change regularly, so check our website for updates.
Our team is dedicated to friendly service, with an emphasis on beer, wine and cocktail knowledge.

We also grow many of our own vegetables in our backyard garden. See what we’re growing here

Goat Town gladly takes reservations, both online and by phone. For parties of 6 or more, give us a call and we will be happy to do our best to accommodate you.
20% gratuity may be added to parties of 6 or more.

Corkage fee is $20 per bottle.

We accept Visa, MasterCard, American Express and Cash. Gift Certificates are also available.
Goat Town is located in the East Village at
511 East 5th Street, between Avenues A and B.
We are located on the north side of the street.

To get here by subway, we are closest to the
1st Ave/14th St L station and the 2nd Ave/E Houston F station.
To get here by bus, take the M14A along Avenue A to East 5th Street, half a block away from Goat Town.
